CMU To Go


OVERVIEW


Our team was challenged to create a responsive website design for the food trucks in the Carnegie Mellon area. To better understand the needs of food truck consumers, we conducted interviews and observations, synthesized our findings, and created personas, scenarios, and storyboards over a two week period.

This research was used to determine how a website could improve consumer experiences. We are now in the process of creating a responsive website design that will inform food truck consumers of whether the truck is open, its typical hours, and give consumers the ability to preorder their food.
