The Challenge

Redesigning the substance addiction recovery experience

Oasis Recovery Center is a non-profit that helps connect those with substance use disorders (SUDs) to local resources (e.g. treatment centers, support group meetings, sober living homes).

As part of our MHCI capstone, our team will be designing a solution that can expand Oasis’s reach and ultimately transform the experience of those that reach out to them for help.

timeline

January - August 2020,
8 months, In Progress

my role

Lead Designer, Project Manager

team

Angelina Thomas, Ben Stone, Connie Chau, Paul Myers

Our Scope

8 months to research and design a solution that integrates with Oasis Recovery Center’s current services

Our process is split into two parts-- a research-focused phase from January to May, and then a design-focused phase from May to August). Though the two halves of the project have different focuses, we will still design prototypes in the research pase and will likewise research in the design phase.
