overview

The Challenge

As an Iontank intern, I was tasked with ideating conceptual prototypes for their large scale, ceiling-hung client installation.

Iontank is a specialized design studio that develops interactive art installations and software systems. Their client project was the lobby centerpiece in a large tech company’s Welcome Center.

I was tasked with creating experimental prototypes to discuss during a client meeting to determine a conceptual direction for the project.

Scope

I had 12 weeks to design, fabricate, and program my prototypes.

Up until the client meeting, I created several different experimental prototypes, largely focusing on forms and materials. After the meeting, I focused on one prototype to polish.

During my internship, I also balanced working on two smaller-scale projects with the Carnegie Museum of Art.

Solution

Light Prototype

This prototype is made of wood and a solid surface material. It contains an Arduino and a strip of LED lights programmed to show different light animations.

Iontank later iterated on this prototype and used it in their final installation. This project will be updated to include photos of the final installation when it’s released on the Iontank website.
