March - April 2019,
6 weeks



TEAM


Fatima Ahmad, David Inyangson, Qinglu Lu



MY ROLE


Lead User Interface Designer,
Co-User Experience Researcher






OVERVIEW


Our team created a mobile app design for shift swapping in the workplace. We conducted user interviews with part-timers and managers in different industries and a design competitive analysis to determine user needs and app scope.

We decided to target medium to large teams and create an app that would fascilitate employee shift swapping with limited time investment from the manager. We focused on creating an in-app calendar, enabling workers to easily visualize schedule conflicts with shifts they want to drop and pick up, a manager approval system, and dropped / picked up shift tracking for managers and workers.
