Hi, I’m Ari. Thanks for stopping by!


I first studied art + coding as a way to use technology to connect with other people. As I became more aware of how entangled technology is with our lives, I increasingly saw digital product design as an important pathway to creating meaningful daily experiences. That led me to design for projects in addiction, cybersecurity, and education, and to get formal training from Carnegie Mellon's Master of HCI program. I now strive to make digital products more inclusive, useful, and beautiful.

When I'm not geeking over design, you can find me reading webtoons, drinking tea, and cooking plant-based meals.
