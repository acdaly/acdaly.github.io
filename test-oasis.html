The Challenge

Oasis Recovery Center challenged us to transform the substance addiction rehabilitation experience by leveraging technology.

Oasis Recovery Center is a non-profit that helps connect those with substance use disorders (SUDs) to local resources (e.g. treatment centers, support group meetings, sober living homes).

Our team will be designing a solution that can expand Oasis’s reach and ultimately transform the experience of those that reach out to them for help.

Our Scope

We have 8 months to design a solution that integrates with Oasis Recovery Center’s current services.

Our process is split into two parts-- a research-focused phase from January to May, and then a design-focused phase from May to August). Though the two halves of the project have different focuses, we will still design prototypes in the research pase and will likewise research in the design phase.

My Role

In our team of 5, we’re all designing prototypes and researching the domain. I am additionally the Project Manager of the team-- I plan our two week sprints to keep us on track with our longer-term roadmap. I’ve also designed our team’s internal design system and presentations.
